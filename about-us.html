About Us

As you can image that our team, FormosaFoodie, is all about information on Taiwanese Food. Born and raised in this island, we feel like the world needs to gain some insight on what Taiwan has to offer. Thus, we have committed ourselves to the mission of spreading the knowledge of Taiwanese cuisines to the rest of the world.

Our Mission

Being a team dedicated to spreading the knowledge of Taiwanese food, our main mission is to make the rest of the world know what Taiwan has in store, especially its food. With our website, you will be able to receive all kind of knowledge of Taiwanese cuisines, including their histories and recipes.
